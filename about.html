Our mission

Making the extraordinary accessible—without cutting corners

We are mountaineers, river guides, rescue technicians, and wilderness medics who refused to choose between thrill and safety. Every Stackly Adventure itinerary is designed around small groups, premium gear, and guides who know the terrain because they've lived it for decades.

  • IFMGA-certified lead guides on every alpine expedition
  • Satellite communications and GPS tracking on all multi-day trips

Our evolution

Milestones on the path to premium adventure

From a single climbing guide collective to a global expedition company—each year sharpened our craft.

2014

The first summit

Three IFMGA guides launched Stackly Adventure with a single Yosemite climbing program—eight adventurers, one unforgettable week.

2016

River & trail expansion

Added kayaking and trail-running programs in the Pacific Northwest, establishing our safety-first briefing protocols.

2018

International alpine routes

Opened Chamonix and Patagonia expeditions with dedicated acclimatization schedules and rescue partnerships.

2020

Adventurer portal launch

Built digital trip tracking, gear checklists, and real-time guide communication for every booked expedition.

2022

Gear partner network

Partnered with leading outdoor brands to provide premium rental kits—harnesses, shells, paddles, and camp systems included.

2024

32 countries, one standard

Reached 2,000 completed expeditions across six adventure disciplines—with a 98% success rate and zero major incidents.

2026

The next horizon

Launching custom corporate wilderness retreats and youth expedition scholarships—adventure for every generation.

How we partner

Your journey, our expertise

From first inquiry to summit celebration—we guide you through every stage with clarity and confidence.

1. We discover your adventure

We start with your experience level, fitness, and dream destinations—not a generic catalog. That shapes route selection, group size, and gear recommendations tailored to you.

2. We prepare you for the wild

Pre-trip briefings, conditioning guides, and gear checklists arrive in your adventurer portal. Your assigned guide is available for questions until departure day.

3. We celebrate every summit

After your expedition, you receive a digital trip recap, photo gallery, and route notes. Many adventurers book their next journey before they even unpack.
