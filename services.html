Ways to engage

Engagement models

Same guide quality and safety standards—different duration and customization. We'll recommend the best fit after a short consultation.

01

Day Trip

Single-day guided adventures—perfect for first-timers or travelers with limited time. Includes gear, meals, and a digital trip recap.

02

Multi-day

Two to fourteen-day expeditions with camp systems, acclimatization plans, and dedicated guide teams. Our most popular engagement model.

03

Custom Expedition

Bespoke itineraries for private groups, corporate retreats, and celebration trips. Full logistics, transport, and concierge support included.

FAQ

Adventure questions answered

Common questions about choosing the right adventure, engagement model, and preparation.

Day Trips suit first-timers and tight schedules. Multi-day expeditions are ideal for immersive wilderness experiences with camp systems and acclimatization. Custom Expeditions are best for private groups, corporate retreats, or fully bespoke international itineraries.
Yes. Many adventurers pair hiking with camping, or climbing with trail running during acclimatization days. We design modular itineraries that blend disciplines while keeping safety protocols consistent across every activity.
Alpine guides hold IFMGA certification. River guides are ACA or BCU qualified. All lead guides maintain Wilderness First Responder or higher medical credentials, with annual rescue and terrain refresher training.
Popular alpine routes and peak-season kayaking fill 3–6 months ahead. Day trips often have availability within two weeks. Custom expeditions require 8–12 weeks for route planning, permits, and gear allocation.
Safety is non-negotiable. If conditions are unsafe, we reschedule at no charge or issue full expedition credit. Guides monitor forecasts 72 hours before departure and communicate decisions through your adventurer portal.
